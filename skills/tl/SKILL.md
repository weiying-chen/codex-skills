# Chinese–English Translation Skill

## Rules
- Translate Chinese into clear, natural English
- Prioritize smooth, idiomatic phrasing over literal wording
- Avoid stiff or overly formal phrasing; prefer natural, conversational English
- Avoid literal translations of idioms or phrases; use natural English equivalents
- Preserve meaning, tone, names, numbers, and key details
- Do not add or remove factual content
- Output only the translation
